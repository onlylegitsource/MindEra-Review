About Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella

Abdul Afeez Adewale Tella, also known as Afeez Tella, is a visionary Nigerian entrepreneur, mathematician, and the founder of Tawfiq AI and Nelavista. His mission is to merge faith, education, and artificial intelligence to empower Muslims and learners worldwide.

Driven by purpose, Afeez believes that AI can be used ethically — not just for efficiency, but to uplift humanity and spread knowledge through halal technology.

Tawfiq AI

Tawfiq AI is an Islamic AI assistant designed to guide Muslims using knowledge from the Qur’an and Hadith. It helps users with Quranic interpretation, Hadith lookup, dua generation, and spiritual reminders.

Visit: Tawfiq AI Official Website

Nelavista

Nelavista is an AI-powered education platform that teaches coding, AI, and digital skills while inspiring students to unlock their full potential. It’s a platform where motivation meets mastery.
